Meet Olivia

A counsellor who shows up with compassion, honesty, and real-world experience.

I became a counsellor because I believe every person deserves someone in their corner — someone who won't flinch, won't judge, and won't give up on them.

My path to this work wasn't a straight line. Long before I had any qualifications, I was learning about resilience, survival, and the profound complexity of being human — through community work, through caring for others, and through life itself. I have sat with people in some of their darkest moments, and I have done my own rebuilding too. That experience shapes everything about how I show up for my clients.

I have worked across inpatient mental health settings — including mood disorder, PTSD, and addiction wards — as well as with children and families in the out-of-home care system, disability support, community services, and crisis support. I bring that breadth of real-world experience into every session.

In the room I am warm, direct, and genuinely present. I will meet you with compassion and without judgment — and I won't shy away from the hard stuff.

I am also a proud mum to a spirited little one, and outside of work you'll find me cooking, creating, or planning the next adventure. I bring that same curiosity and love of discovery into my work — because I believe the journey of knowing yourself is one of the greatest adventures there is.

I started The Ignite Practice because I wanted to create something that felt true — a space where people from all walks of life, all backgrounds, all stories, could come and be truly met. Whoever you are and whatever you are carrying — you belong here.

It is an honour to do this work. I don't take that lightly.
